Madeon announces new merch drop on online store, includes exclusive items from Red Rocks/Shrine.

Dec 1, 2023

At 12 pm ET, Madeon announced the final merch drop of the Good Faith era for his online merch store. The drop includes exclusive items previously only sold at the Good Faith Forever shows at Red Rocks and The Shrine. Among these items are the test screen flag, hoodie, and shirt, the Gonna Be Good tee and hat, the Red Rocks hoodie, and the final bow Shrine poster. Restocked items include the GBG zine bundle, as well as restocks of various older items.

Madeon makes guest appearance at ISOxo's LA Shrine Show.

Madeon made a surprise appearance during ISOxo's day 1 set of him and Knock2's multi-day series of Shows at LA's Shrine venue. BREAKING: Fan recieves rare imperial pendant at Good Faith Forever at The Shrine.

Nov 17, 2023

An anonymous Madeon fan recieved one of the elusive imperial pendants during a never seen before "ritual" on stage during Good Faith Forever at The Shrine. The fan was selected after taking part in a brief ARG that started with a teaser at the Red Rocks show. The pendants, which were revealed to the world all the way back in mid-2019, were originally concieved to be found by Madeon fans worldwide, but were never able to be located by fans. This is the first known time that anyone who is not Madeon or one of his associates has ever been in possession of a pendant. The "ritual" for passing on the pendant at the final Good Faith Forever show may be symbolic for the end of the Good Faith Era.

The "Good Faith" album turns four years old today.

Nov 15, 2023

Good Faith, Madeon's sophmore album, celebrates its fourth birthday today. Originally released on november 15, 2019, the context of the album has changed significantly since it's inception, with multiple variations of liveshows, new singles, a secret ARG and EP project, and countless other pieces of related content have evolved what was once just an album into a full on "Good Faith Era", which is set to close soon with the final Good Faith Forever show taking place in two days at The Shrine in LA.

Madeon unveils livestream for rescheduled Red Rocks show.

Nov 9, 2023

Madeon has revealed on Twitter that he will be livestreaming his Good Faith Forever show at Red Rocks on Youtube. The stream will be available for all to watch and was created to make up for all of those who traveled for his original Red Rocks show on October 29, which was unfortunately postponed due to weather concerns. This will also be the first time that Good Faith Forever will be streamed on Madeon's offical Youtube channel. Fans of Madeon around the world, especially those who traveled out to Denver for the first show and could not travel back out again, will be delighted to experience Good Faith Forever in their own homes.
